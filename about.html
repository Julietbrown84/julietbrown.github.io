My creative process often seeks the nature of truth. Being inventive is important, as I believe that being curious and asking questions are practice seeks the nature of truth. Being inventive is important, she believes that being curious and asking questions are new ways to see, surprise, excite and delight. Her philosophical viewpoint, believes where language ends true creativity begins It’s all part of a language that we think we know. Creativity for me is not the ability to create out of nothing, but the ability to generate new ideas by combining, changing, or reapplying existing ideas. In a dynamic and changing world, the challenges of man are also not static. This is to go beyond the mundane and the obvious and reject the traps of repetition and pre- set categories which challenges current beliefs and mind sets including her own.
